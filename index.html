What is Whomst-Host?


Whomst-Host is a client side webhost for single page websites.
If you want to quickly get a demo or landing page setup, you can do so in one click. Simply paste your code in the box, and click the button.

How does it Work?


Unlike most webhosts, Whomst-Host stores your website's source code on the client side (in the URL). This means that it does not require any storage on our end, and no sign-up on your end.
